Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage or cancel your subscription anytime from Settings within the app, or through your Apple or Google account subscription settings directly.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds for subscriptions are handled by Apple or Google directly, since they process the payment. You can request a refund through your Apple ID account (reportaproblem.apple.com) or your Google Play order history.

How do I delete my data or my account?

Go to Settings within the app and select "Delete My Data." This permanently removes your account and all associated data, including your reading lists and any community content you've posted. This cannot be undone.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings within the app and select "Export My Data" to download a copy of your reading lists and account information.

How do I report inappropriate content or a user?

Email us at tmrw.pod@gmail.com with details (a screenshot helps), and we'll review it promptly. See our Terms of Use for more on community guidelines.

A title I'm looking for isn't in Fixated. Can you add it?

Email us the title and we'll look into adding it. Fixated is actively growing its library based on what the community is reading.

Where can I actually read the titles Fixated recommends?

Fixated links out to official, licensed reading platforms (such as Webtoon, Lezhin, Tappytoon, and Manta) for each title. We don't host reading content ourselves.
