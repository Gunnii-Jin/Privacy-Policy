Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Manage or cancel your subscription anytime from Settings within the app, or through your Apple/Google account subscription settings.

How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings within the app and select "Delete My Data." This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
